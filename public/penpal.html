PenPal

Screens

Description:

PenPal is a series of mockups I created for the concept of a social meeting app as my Senior Project at State University of New York at Purchase College. The aim of PenPal is to help international students traveling abroad to meet local students prior to their arrival. The core of the project was based at Purchase College but the mockups reflect my experience studying in Copenhagen. I created the branding for the app. I choose PenPal because I thought it would re-appropriate a term we know to be something of the past. As for the logo I thought using typography would be a good choice at the time but now I believe a symbol may be been a better choice. It would be something recognizable and memorable while being conveyed through all languages.
