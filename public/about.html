Here's A Lil About Me

Designer + Coder

Hi there, I’m Leah. I’m a web designer and technology enthusiast who strives to create clean and dynamic user interfaces. I have over 4 years experience designing and building web pages. I’m also an amateur photographer and an audio engineer. My education and international travel experiences have molded me to be a motivated self-starter, digital artist, and a team player.

I am a creative person; whether it is designing an app to solve a problem or creating new recipes in the kitchen. I enjoy researching and understanding how things work. I’m a designer who is passionate about the web and all things related. I enjoy collaborative projects because when ideas are fused even greater ones are produced.

Growing up in the Caribbean and traveling have helped me to look at design through various lens. In life, I’ve learned that I will never know it all, therefore I’m always eager to learn more and grow through experience. When I’m not busy modifying my code or redesigning an old project you can find me cycling, listening to podcasts or binge watching a show.
