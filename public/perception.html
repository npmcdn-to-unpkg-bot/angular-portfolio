Description:

Perception: Innernational? is the focal point of my BA Senior Project. It focuses on the integration of international students at State University of New York at Purchase College campus. I decided to study the experiences of students studying abroad. This project is based on some of my own experiences prior to my studying in Denmark and those of students studying abroad at SUNY at Purchase College. My findings include information obtained from library research and the experiences of the students studying at SUNY at Purchase during 2014-2015 academic year. It inspired me to create certain tools to evaluate the experiences of students studying outside their native culture.

I met with numerous students studying abroad at the college and interviewed them. They told me about their expectations of the college and America prior to their arrival. I chose expectations/perceptions because I believe that it would be interesting to hear foreignerâ€™s perspective of America before experiencing it.
