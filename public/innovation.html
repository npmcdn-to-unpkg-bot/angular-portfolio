Quritiba

Screens

Description:

Innovation Embassy is a Danish company that helps individuals to make their ideas a reality through crowdsourcing talents. During my study abroad experience in Copenhagen, Denmark the company came in as a client and pitched the task of redesigning their user interface. We were tasked with simplifying the sign-up process and making real-time collaboration easier. We eliminated some of the sign-up steps by integrating the use of social media and creating a platform that encouraged simultaneous work.

This was the product of my team Buzzlink. The other designer and I split the workload in half in order to meet the deadline. Not all screens are depicted.
