Description:

Buzzlink is a student-peer company created during my time studying abroad at the Copenhagen School of Design and Technology in Copenhagen, Denmark. In this collaborative team had different talents, I doubled as project manager and assistant designer. We needed a corporate identity to represent us when interacting with clients. My designs were selected from among the team as our identity.
